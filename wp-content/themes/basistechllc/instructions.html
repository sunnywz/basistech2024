Spacing Classes
block-spacing-mega Top and bottom spacing (very large)
block-spacing-double Top and bottom spacing double
block-spacing-top-double Top spacing doubles
block-spacing-bottom-double Bottom spacing doubles
block-spacing-half Top and bottom spacing halve
block-spacing-top-half Top spacing halves
block-spacing-bottom-half Bottom spacing halves
block-spacing-none Remove top and bottom spacing completely
block-spacing-top-none Remove top spacing completely
block-spacing-bottom-none Remove bottom spacing completely
Styling Classes
home-hero Used for home page hero custom styling (very large h1 and larger p's, negative margin on accent)
interior-hero Used for interio hero custom styling (larger h1's and p's, negative margin on accent)
intro-circle Yellow moving circle
offset-accent Offset accent image (apply negative top margin so that it appears above this block)
banner-cta Used for home page banner custom styling
valign-center In a two-column layout, use this to center the columns vertically. (By default, they are aligned to the top.)
valign-bottom In a two-column layout, use this to align the columns to the bottom vertically. (By default, they are aligned to the top.)
large-paragraphs Make paragraphs 1.25rem with a condensed line height.
intro-paragraphs Make paragraphs 1.5rem and normal line height.
blue-headers Make headers dark blue and bold
sidebar-first-column Makes first column appear as a light gray bar
sidebar-last-column Makes last column appear as a light gray bar
